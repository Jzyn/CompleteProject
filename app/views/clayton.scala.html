The Clayton



This property is 15 minutes walk from the beach. Boasting spacious rooms with en suite bathrooms, Clayton Hotel, Ballsbridge is a beautifully restored 19th-century Masonic orphan’s school with landscaped gardens. Located less than 5 minutes' walk from the RDS, the city buses stop right outside the hotel. Underground car parking is available for an additional charge.


Bedrooms feature fluffy duvets and hypo-allergenic pillows. Rooms are sound proofed for a quiet nights’ sleep and have desks with lamps and free Wi-Fi.


Traditional breakfasts are served each morning, with light snacks and a carvery through the day. In the evening, international cuisine is served in the Brasserie Restaurant.


With its stained glass windows, high ceilings, oak panelled walls and original mosaic tiling, the restored Thomas Prior Hall is the jewel in the crown of this beautiful hotel.


With the 3Arena nearby, Tom’s Bar is the perfect place for a pre-concert drinks and food. The Aviva Stadium, Bord Gais Energy Theatre and the Grand Canal are within 1.6 km of Clayton Hotel, Ballsbridge.
